24 Jan
Patch 1.8
Changelog:
New Feature: Plasma Gun
Enemies from the Tokyo levels now have a chance to drop Plasma Guns! Plasma Guns can hold up to 24 shots of ammo. Ammo charges will drop in small amounts from human enemies and in big amounts from robotic enemies.
New Level: Tokyo Slums
Embark on a journey in the lowest district of Tokyo’s futuristic landscape. Meet old foes, two new enemy types and tons of ramen bowls!
31 Oct
Patch 1.7
Changelog:
New Level: Lost Angeles
Explore the US city equiped with the most cybernetic technology. Face off robotic enemies coming from times as old as World War III. Be careful though, as prolonged maintainance can only do so much for any piece of technology! These archaic constructs will occasionally malfunction, at times giving you an easier time beating them, and in other cases even explode, putting you into a bigger danger!
Bugfix #74:
Jumping over certain enemies with flexible model sizes will no longer cause damage to player when it shouldn’t.
